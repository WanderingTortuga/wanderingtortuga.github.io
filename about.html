Wandering Tortuga Studio began with a fascination for the landscapes and hidden treasures found throughout the American West.

Growing up in Florida, rocks and minerals always felt like something from another world. Obsidian especially seemed almost mythical — a volcanic stone from places far away from the flat landscapes I knew.

Over time, collecting became less about owning stones and more about the experience of finding them. Every piece carries the memory of where it was found, the landscape around it, and the forces that shaped it over thousands or millions of years.

The stones featured here are personally collected whenever permitted and chosen carefully. Ethical sourcing means respecting the land, avoiding over-collection, and knowing the story behind the material.

Wandering Tortuga Studio is a small journey of discovery — one stone, one adventure, and one creation at a time.

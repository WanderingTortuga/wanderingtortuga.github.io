About the Journey

Wandering Tortuga Studio is built from a life spent moving across the American West, gathering stones along the way. Each piece comes from a moment, a place, and a journey.

This is a growing collection—raw materials shaped slowly into cabochons and, eventually, handmade copper jewelry through ongoing study in lapidary and metalsmithing.
